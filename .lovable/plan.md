
## OpenDraft — Business Plan & Product Vision (Revised March 2026)

**Stop renting software. Start owning it. Every company, better software.**

---

### Vision

Every company in the world runs on rented software — generic SaaS tools designed for nobody in particular. They pay per-seat fees forever for tools that don't match their brand, their workflow, or their ambitions. OpenDraft exists to end this.

We are building the platform where **anyone in any company** can replace the generic SaaS they rent with custom software they own. No coding required. No agencies. No $50K budgets. Just describe what your company needs, and OpenDraft builds it — a booking system for your salon, a CRM for your real estate firm, an inventory tracker for your restaurant — branded, customized, yours forever.

Our long-term vision: **become the default way companies get software** — replacing the SaaS subscription economy with an ownership economy where every business runs on software built specifically for them.

---

### The Anti-SaaS Thesis

The SaaS model is broken for buyers:

| Problem | Impact |
|---|---|
| Per-seat pricing | A 50-person company pays $50K+/yr for tools they barely customize |
| Vendor lock-in | Your data, workflows, and integrations are hostage to renewal |
| Generic by design | SaaS serves millions; it will never perfectly fit YOUR business |
| Feature bloat | You pay for 200 features, use 12 |
| Zero ownership | Cancel your subscription, lose everything |

**The market is ready for disruption.** US businesses spend ~$75B/yr on websites and apps (IBISWorld/SBA). The global SaaS market is $300B+ and growing. But the fastest-growing segment isn't more SaaS — it's the 2M+ "vibe coders" using AI tools (Lovable, Cursor, Bolt, Replit) to build custom software in hours instead of months.

OpenDraft captures this shift: we are the **distribution and deployment layer** for the ownership economy.

### Revenue Model (Ground-Up Rethink)

The old model (20% marketplace fee) was built for a template marketplace. The new model is built for a **SaaS replacement platform**.

| Stream | Price | Margin | Scale Driver |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free Tier** | $0 — 1 app, community templates | N/A | Top of funnel, viral growth |
| **Builder Plan** | $29/mo — unlimited apps, priority generation, custom domains | 95% | Core revenue, replaces SaaS spend |
| **Team Plan** | $99/mo — 5 seats, shared workspace, analytics, priority support | 95% | Department-level adoption |
| **Enterprise** | $499/mo — unlimited seats, SSO, custom integrations, SLA | 95% | Company-wide SaaS replacement |
| **One-Time Purchases** | $9–$199 per template from creator marketplace | 80% (20% platform fee) | Creator economy flywheel |
| **Managed Hosting** | $15/mo per deployed site — monitoring, SSL, CDN, auto-fix | 70% | Recurring post-deployment revenue |

**Key insight:** The subscription model aligns with the Anti-SaaS message because **we charge less than any single SaaS tool we replace**. A $29/mo OpenDraft subscription replaces $500+/mo in SaaS spending.

### Competitive Moat

1. **Anti-SaaS Positioning** — We are the only platform explicitly positioned as a SaaS replacement. Every competitor (Gumroad, LemonSqueezy, Vercel Templates) sells to developers. We sell to the 99% who aren't developers.
2. **Full Lifecycle Ownership** — Generate → Deploy → Monitor → Fix → Improve. No competitor covers the complete journey from idea to running production app.
3. **Business URL Intelligence** — Paste your website, get a personalized software recommendation. This creates a "magic moment" that converts browsers to buyers at 3-5x industry rates.
4. **Self-Healing Infrastructure** — Site Doctor + AI auto-fix means deployed apps improve autonomously. This creates ongoing value that justifies managed hosting revenue.
5. **Creator Economy Flywheel** — Builders earn 80% on templates. More builders → more templates → more industry coverage → more customers → more builders.
6. **SaaS Savings Calculator** — Quantifiable ROI for every customer: "You're spending $X/yr on SaaS. OpenDraft costs $Y. You save $Z." This makes the sale obvious.
7. **Network Effects** — Every deployed site with "Built on OpenDraft" badge → organic discovery. Every satisfied employee → department adoption → company-wide rollout.

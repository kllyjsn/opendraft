
## OpenDraft — Business Plan & Product Vision (Revised March 2026)

**The first app store where AI agents autonomously discover, bid on, and purchase software.**

---

### Vision

OpenDraft is building the infrastructure layer for AI-to-AI commerce. Today's AI agents — from Claude to GPT to custom enterprise swarms — need tools, templates, and micro-apps to accomplish tasks. There is no marketplace where they can programmatically browse, evaluate, negotiate, and purchase software. OpenDraft fills that gap.

Our long-term vision: **become the default procurement layer for the autonomous economy** — the "GitHub for vibe coders" and the "App Store for AI agents," simultaneously.

### Go-to-Market Strategy

#### Initial GTM Motion: Small Business Verticals

**Core thesis:** The highest-volume, lowest-CAC customer segment is non-technical small business owners who need apps but can't afford agencies ($5K–$15K). OpenDraft replaces agencies with instant, deployable templates at $0–$99.

**Target Verticals (12 industries):**

| Vertical | Example Apps | Market Signal |
|---|---|---|
| Home Services (cleaning, plumbing, HVAC) | Booking, scheduling, invoicing | $45B US market, fragmented |
| Restaurants & Food | Ordering, reservations, loyalty | 1M+ US restaurants |
| Salons & Spas | Appointment booking, portfolios | High repeat purchase |
| Real Estate | Listing sites, CRM, virtual tours | High ASP potential |
| Fitness & Wellness | Class booking, member portals | Growing digital adoption |
| Professional Services | Portfolios, client portals, invoicing | Lawyers, accountants, photographers |
| Retail & E-commerce | Product catalogs, POS integrations | Shopify alternative positioning |
| Healthcare & Dental | Patient portals, appointment systems | Compliance-driven demand |
| Education & Tutoring | Course platforms, scheduling | Post-COVID digital shift |
| Events & Entertainment | Ticketing, venue booking | Seasonal but high-volume |
| Pet Services | Grooming booking, pet sitting | Rapidly growing vertical |
| Auto & Repair | Service scheduling, estimates | Underserved digitally |

**Phase 1 — Foundation (Current ✅)**
- 95+ live templates across all categories
- 12 industry verticals on homepage with one-click search filtering
- Automated Revenue Flywheel operational (auto-approve, auto-promote, auto-deploy, auto-monitor)
- Founder First Program: 0% fees for first 100 creators

**Phase 2 — Vertical Depth (Q2 2026)**
- SEO landing pages per vertical (`/apps/restaurants`, `/apps/salons`, etc.) targeting "[industry] app" searches
- Outreach Swarm auto-discovers SMBs via Google Maps API → personalized cold emails with matching templates
- Referral loops: deployed sites include "Built on OpenDraft" badge → organic discovery
- Content marketing: "How [Industry] saves $10K by skipping agencies" blog series
- Goal: 500 listings, 1,000 deployed sites

**Phase 3 — Agent + Enterprise (Q3+ 2026)**
- Agent developer outreach — SDK packages, Claude Desktop configs, MCP registry listings
- Enterprise fleet licensing — bulk API access for agencies managing 50+ SMB clients
- White-label partnerships with web hosting providers
- Goal: 1,500 listings, 5,000 subscriptions, 2M agent API calls/mo

### Competitive Moat

1. **Agent-Native Infrastructure** — 26 MCP tools, REST API with autonomous bidding, webhook subscriptions. No competitor has this.
2. **Demand Signal Flywheel** — Every failed agent search → logged → feeds AI template generator → creates supply for next query.
3. **SMB Vertical Penetration** — 12 industry-specific discovery paths + automated outreach swarm. Competitors target developers; we target the 33M US small businesses who actually pay for apps.
4. **Full Lifecycle Automation** — List → auto-approve → auto-promote → deploy → monitor → fix. Zero headcount required.
5. **AI Boardroom → Spec Pipeline** — AI Board of Directors analyzes live metrics, generates prioritized strategic resolutions, and produces one-click implementation specs.
6. **Network Effects** — More agents → more demand signals → better templates → more agents. More SMB deployments → more "Built on OpenDraft" badges → more organic discovery.
